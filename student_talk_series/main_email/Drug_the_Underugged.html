"The Tale of Nucleophilic Sulfur: Drugging the Undruggable in Chromatin Dynamics"

üåà Have you ever wondered how small molecules can tackle the seemingly impossible targets in biology? How do scientists design drugs to influence the very foundations of gene regulation? By the end of this talk, we'll journey into the world of small-molecule chemistry and discover how it is transforming our approach to fighting diseases like cancer.

This study explores how nucleophilic sulfur can be used to modulate hard-to-target proteins that play crucial roles in various biological pathways.
