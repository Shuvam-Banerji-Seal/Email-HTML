The Beginner's Guide to Machine General Intelligence

Greetings, IISER Kolkata.

While the accessibility of modern AI tools has grown rapidly, understanding their underlying architecture remains paramount. We invite you to a comprehensive study of the Mathematical Foundations of Machine Intelligence. This workshop is designed to transition participants from passive users to informed architects of AI systems.

Day 1: Origins

Counting numbers to LLM Inference. Classifiers, Resource Mapping, and Image Generation from scratch.

Day 2: The Future

Mastering Boosting Algorithms and building autonomous Agents that act on your behalf.

The Prerequisites

Before we hit the GPU, verify your foundation. These tracks cover the path from Assembly & Compilers to the logic needed for Neural Networks.
